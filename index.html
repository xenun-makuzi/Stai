Our Story

About MarioLogistics

Pioneering the future of global logistics through technology, trust, and radical transparency.

Founded 2015

Delivering Trust Since Day One

MarioLogistics was founded on a single belief: logistics should be transparent, reliable, and stress-free for everyone involved — sender, receiver, and operator alike.

What started as a small regional courier tracker has grown into an enterprise-grade global platform trusted by thousands of businesses across 150+ countries.

Our team of engineers, logistics experts, and customer success specialists work around the clock to ensure every package reaches its destination — on time, every time.
